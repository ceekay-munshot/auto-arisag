Vehicle Registrations

Vaahan is the official digital platform of the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India, dedicated to maintaining a comprehensive national vehicle registry. The platform centralizes data pertaining to registered vehicles across India. On its public dashboard, "parivahan," users can access detailed statistics on monthly vehicle registrations segmented by vehicle type, fuel type, make, and other parameters. Additionally, it offers insights into vehicle fitness statuses, permits, and other related services. This data serves as an invaluable resource for stakeholders looking to understand trends and patterns in India's vehicular landscape.
