Category-wise Electric Vehicle Sales, Mar 2026 | India

Total Registered Electric Vehicle Sales – Mar’26 – 2,79,530 I Feb’26 – 1,95,793

Source: Vahan Dashboard as of Apr 2, 2026. Telangana Data not included. Low speed e-2W sales data not included.

Electric vehicle sales in India showed strong growth across most categories in March 2026 compared to February 2026. E2W sales rose sharply to 1,91,067 units from 1,12,628, while E4W sales increased to 22,315 from 14,712. E3W L5 Passenger also grew to 23,468 units from 21,107, and E3W L5 Cargo/Goods increased to 3,710 from 3,193. E-goods carrier sales climbed to 1,885 from 1,458, and e-carts saw a marginal rise to 7,424 from 7,268. However, e-rickshaw sales declined to 28,942 units from 34,831, and e-bus sales dipped slightly to 559 from 578. The “Others” category recorded a notable increase to 160 units from 18.

Category wise-Sales Trend from Mar 2025 to Mar 2026

24,52,056 EVs sold in last 12 months from Apr 2025 to Mar 2026

Source: Vahan Dashboard as of Apr 2, 2026. Telangana Data not included. Low speed e-2W sales data not included.

India recorded 24,52,056 EV sales from April 2025 to March 2026, per Vahan Dashboard data (excluding Telangana and low-speed e-2Ws). Monthly sales peaked in March 2026 (2,79,530) and were lowest in April 2025 (1,69,355).

Fuel-wise 2-Wheeler Sales Trend, Mar 2025 – Mar 2026

Source: Vahan Dashboard as of Apr 2, 2026. Telangana Data not included. Low speed e-2W sales data not included.

EV penetration in the two-wheeler (2W) category increased from 6.6% in February 2026 to 9.8% in March 2026.

The penetration of electric vehicles (EVs) in the four-wheeler passenger car/cab category rose from 3.7% in February 2026 to 5.1% in March 2026.
