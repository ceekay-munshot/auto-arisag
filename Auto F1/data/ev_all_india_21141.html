Dataset Overview

This Dataset contains all india level, year, month, fuel norms and fuel type wise total number of Electric Vehicles (EV) and Hybrid Vehicles Registered Note: 1. Fuel Types Considered for Electric Vehicles are Diesel/Hybrid, Electric (Battery Operated Vehicle), Petrol/Hybrid, Plug-in Hybrid EV, Pure EV, Strong Hybrid EV, Fuel Cell Hydrogen are as per https://e-amrit.niti.gov.in/types-of-electric-v...
